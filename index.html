Our Story

Grooming With Heart

The Groom Room 101 was built on one belief: every pet deserves the same love and care as family. We're passionate groomers who treat each animal as our own.

We specialize in all breeds, sizes, and temperaments — including anxious and senior pets. Our calm, clean space keeps your pet comfortable from start to finish. Dogs, cats, rabbits, ferrets — all welcome!
